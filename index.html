Welcome to Chengdu

CHENGDU, a city known for its delicious cuisine, adorable giant pandas, and exquisite Shu embroidery, is a vibrant destination filled with unique cultural charm. Famous for its spicy Sichuan dishes like hotpot and mapo tofu, Chengdu is a paradise for food lovers. As the hometown of the giant panda, it also hosts a renowned panda breeding center, drawing visitors from around the world. Additionally, Chengdu is celebrated for Shu embroidery, a traditional craft that showcases intricate designs and rich cultural heritage. Together, these elements make Chengdu an unforgettable city blending tradition with modern appeal.

The Three "Treasures" of Chengdu

HOTPOT

Chengdu's hotpot is famous for its rich, spicy broth and customizable ingredients, offering a unique and fiery dining experience that reflects Sichuan's love for bold flavors.

SHU XIU

Shu embroidery, a traditional craft from Chengdu, is renowned for its intricate designs and vibrant colors, showcasing the exquisite skill and cultural heritage of Sichuan artisans."

PANDA

Pandas: "Giant pandas, native to Sichuan, are cherished worldwide, and Chengdu is home to a world-famous research base dedicated to the preservation and care of this beloved species."
